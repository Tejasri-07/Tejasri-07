Aditya Institute of Technology and Management (AITAM College) is an Engineering and Management college located in Tekkali, Srikakulam District of Andhra Pradesh State. The college was established in 2001. AITAM offers six undergraduate and post graduate programs in varied disciplines like IT, CSE, Mechanical, Civil, Electronics, and Electrical Engineering.
